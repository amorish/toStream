Privacy Policy

Our Commitment

Your privacy is fundamental to our service. We built this platform with security in mind from the ground up, ensuring that your communications remain entirely private and accessible only to you and your selected participants.

Peer to Peer Communication

All video and audio streams are transmitted directly between you and your partner using WebRTC technology. Your media streams are encrypted end to end and never pass through or get recorded on our central servers. We have absolutely no technical ability to access your live streams.

Information We Collect

We collect only the essential information required to operate the service. This includes your account credentials, basic profile details, and room metadata necessary for connection signaling. We do not track your browsing habits or sell your personal data to third parties.

Data Retention

Room identifiers and metadata are stored temporarily to facilitate connections and generate your personal dashboard history. Account data is retained until you choose to delete your account, at which point all associated records are permanently erased from our databases.

Security Measures

We implement strict security protocols to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, or destruction. We regularly review our security practices to ensure your data remains safe.
